# neon flow pro: finance tracker

Stop the "Checkout Regret" Before It Happens

Ever hit "Place Order" and immediately felt that sting in your bank account? I built NeonFlow Pro to solve that.

It’s not just another finance app you’ll forget to open. It’s a Chrome extension that lives where you shop.

🔵Aesthetic UI: because finance shouldn't be boring.

⚪️The Interceptor: If you're on a checkout page and the item puts you over your daily limit, it sends a real-time alert to your desktop.


🔵Data Control: One-click CSV export to track your habits like a pro.
